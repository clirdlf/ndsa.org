National Digital Stewardship Alliance

Establish, maintain, and advance the capacity to preserve our nation's digital resources for the benefit of present and future generations.

Learn More

about

The National Digital Stewardship Alliance is a consortium of 165 organizations, including universities, government and nonprofit organizations, commercial businesses, and professional associations, that are committed to the long-term preservation of digital information. Members collaborate to preserve access to our national digital heritage.
